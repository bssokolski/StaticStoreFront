Resevoir Dogs

A 1992 American neo-noircrime film written and directed by Quentin Tarantino in his feature-length debut.

In the Mood for Love

A 2000 Hong Kong romantic drama film. It tells the story of a man and a woman whose spouses have an affair together and who slowly develop feelings for each other.

Suspiria

A 1977 Italian supernatural horror film. The film stars Jessica Harper as an American ballet student who transfers to a prestigious dance academy in Germany but realizes, after a series of brutal murders, that the academy is a front for a supernatural conspiracy.
